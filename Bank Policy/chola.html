Policy Features & Guidelines

Main policy points presented in CRM readable format.

Chola Personal Loan — Delhi NCR

Loan amount, tenure calculation, credit card outstanding and balance transfer points.

Policy Features

Major USPs

₹30L Max LA84M CalculationROI 13.5%A / B / Govt
Loan Amount: Rs. 30L. Salary should be 1.5L, JCP 5 years, working with A, B and Govt.
84 Months Calculations: Eligibility will be calculated on 84 months.
CC OS: 3 times credit card outstanding from gross salary will not be obligated.
ROI: 13.5% valid till 30th April-26 for Government and Cat A profiles.

BT & Inquiry Rules

Inquiries: 5 unique inquiries required in last 30 days. Top-up inquiry can be ignored.
Tracks: 6 tracks are allowed for BT, PL/OD/DOD/CC and 6 MOB to be paid.
BT Credit Card Outstanding: Up to 8 times allowed for balance transfer.
BT Rule: PL / OD / DOD / CC can be considered under track rule with 6 MOB paid condition.

Calculation Rules
-1 Govt EmployeeLoan amount can be funded up to 20L.
