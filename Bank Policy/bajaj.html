Full Underwriting · Core Policy Parameters
Policy ParameterPolicy Norms
Customer Profile
  • Salaried employees working in companies / Government are allowed.
  • Contractual employees are not allowed.
  • Employees of proprietorships, partnerships, HUF, trusts and co-operative societies are not to be funded.
  • Applicant should not be promoter of the company or partner in a firm.
  • No funding for customer staying in PG, hostel or guest house.
Age Norms
  • Minimum age: 23 years at loan application. Age below 23 is not allowed in any program.
  • Maximum age: 59 years at loan maturity.
  • Age >=23 and <25: CIBIL <720 not allowed, Red segment not allowed, salary >75k required, and HL ever / owned house condition applies.
  • If age >55, tenure must be adjusted so loan expires at 59 years.
  • Growth market age >55: salary must be >=75k.
  • Govt employee with retirement proof: maximum age up to 62 at maturity.
  • Professors in Govt / University: age up to 65 at maturity.
  • BSNL employee max age: 50 years.
MCP Norms / Instant Reject
  • On-us unsecured loans with MOB <4 months are not allowed.
  • On-us history of restructuring ever is not allowed.
  • On-us settlement ever is not allowed.
  • Written-off amount >= ₹2,000 is not allowed.
  • On-us MFI customers are not allowed.
  • >=25 all bureau enquiries in last 12 months are not allowed.
  • Bucket >0 in Auto Finance is not allowed.
  • Wilful Defaulter / Suit Filed Wilful Default in CIBIL is not allowed.
Salary Norms
  • Prime and Annex locations: minimum net salary 50k.
  • Growth locations: minimum net salary 35k.
Banking & Salary Slip
  • Salary bank account only.
  • Minimum 3 months bank statement.
  • Salary credits must be on regular dates; 5 working days fluctuation allowed.
  • EMI bounce in last 3 months to be checked; technical bounce can be accepted.
  • Salary credit should be direct mode and not cash / cheque.
  • Super Green & Green: 1 month salary slip.
  • Amber / Red: 3 months salary slips.
  • Salary slip not required in NIP program.
Employment Experience
  • Total employment experience >=3 years; not applicable for age 23 to <25 years.
  • Current employment >=1 day.
  • If current employment <1 month and salary slip not available: CTC letter, email confirmation, previous employer salary banking and validation required.
Eligibility CalculationEligibility = Minimum of FOIR, Multiplier and Policy Capping.

Special 24x / 26x Enhancement
  • Where 22x is currently applicable, it can be enhanced to 24x.
  • Where 24x is currently applicable, it can be enhanced to 26x.

  • Not applicable for Bhubaneshwar & Kolkata.
Multiplier Restrictions
  • >=3 PL/BL including BFL: multiplier capped at 20x irrespective of salary and segment.
  • Unlisted company category: flat 12x across all risk segments.
  • Top C1 companies: 24x applicable for Super Green and Green in salary-based programs except Employee Loan and MTS.
  • Listed company Super Green/Green with unsecured loan <3, ETB enquiry <9 and salary >75k: +2x.
  • Listed company Super Green/Green with unsecured loan <3, NTB enquiry <5 and salary >75k: +2x.
FOIR Kicker Set 1
  • ABB >40k.
  • Salary >1L in Prime OR salary >75k in Growth.
  • Super Green OR Green segment.
  • Not applicable in MTS and ABB program.
FOIR Kicker Set 2
  • Super Green, salary >75k, Ever HL in bureau.
  • Live unsecured loans <2.
  • Listed company only; excludes Unlisted, Pvt Ltd category, Delisted, Unlisted LLP, Central/State Entity, School/College/Hospitals.
  • ABB >=20k.
  • Not applicable in MTS and SAL Government.
75% FOIR Set 3
  • Green segment, salary >75k, Ever HL in bureau, live unsecured loans <2.
  • Listed company only with ABB >=20k.
  • Not applicable in MTS and SAL Government.
FOIR Restrictions
  • Normal BT & CC BT with >=3 PL including BFL and no HL: FOIR restricted to 55%.
  • >=3 PL/BL including BFL: FOIR cap applies irrespective of salary.

15.3 Bounce & Delinquency Norms
  • Current bucket should be 0.
  • No bounce in last 3 months; should be paid within 10 days.
  • On-us write-off <100 allowed without deviation; >=100 to 2000 with vintage >3 years and ZCM deviation; >2000 not allowed.
  • On-us restructure not allowed.
  • On-us settled not allowed.
  • VAS product bounce not considered for exclusion.
15.4 BFL Unsecured Loan Norms
  • Existing SALPL or BFL/BFSD unsecured customer allowed after 4 MOB under full underwriting norms.
  • Customer cannot have more than 1 live SALPL loan with BFL; existing SALPL requires Top-Up only, no parallel funding except securitization LANs.
  • BFSD obligation to be considered in FOIR.
  • Live BFL PLCS customers can be funded in SAL PL, but PLCS EMI to be obligated.
  • Max 3 live unsecured BFL loans including BFSD.
  • Max 7 live BFL loans excluding secured mortgage/CC/GL/VAS.
15.5 Marginal Segment Norms
  • Core BAU includes FUW/NIP, Normal BT & CC BT, 100% BT, ABB, Employee Loan, Unlisted LLP, DOE1, LINE, SAL ETB DOE.
  • >=6 PL/BL not allowed in any program.
  • >=4 PL/BL in Amber/Red with salary <2L not allowed for SALPL ETB.
  • CC BT & Normal BT with >=3 PL and no HL: FOIR restricted to 55%.
  • >=3 PL/BL not allowed in stock broking/securities or NBFC companies.
  • Delhi/NCR, Kolkata, Hyderabad/Mumbai and other enquiry-specific restrictions apply as listed in policy.
15.6 Risk SegmentationRisk segment is derived from crossbanding of analytical risk segment and PL score. Revised risk segments used are Super Green, Green, Amber, Red and Dark Red.
15.7 Employment Check
  • Official email ID confirmation and domain check OR positive EPFO OR TAN check for Government employees OR online employment check for Government employees OR RSA.
  • Email mandatory for previous cases processed with email and no employer change.
  • PF deduction in payslip requires EPFO check to be positive.
  • Generic office/HR mail confirmation not allowed.
15.8 Bank Verification Norms
  • If Account Aggregator or Perfios Net Banking is done, no covert required.
  • If AA fails: use OCR / Perfios statement upload / covert depending on EPDF availability.
  • Perfios fraud status requires AA or Net Banking Perfios.
  • Physical statements require covert verification as per policy.
15.9 Geo Tagging Norms
  • Geo tagging required for Bureau No-Hit, manpower sourcing company, approved LA >=20L, and cases not meeting waiver norms.
  • Waiver possible if LA <20L, current address proof is not DOVD, salary >=75k, Super Green/Green, HL ever or electricity bill in applicant name, and employment confirmation/TAN check done.
15.10 Identity Checks
  • PAN validation mandatory; PAN should be existing and valid.
  • NSDL PAN result to be checked for each case.
  • Name should match SFDC name.
  • CIBIL PAN should match application PAN.
  • CIBIL DOB/PAN mismatch requires external CIBIL verification.
  • Mobile mismatch with bureau requires UPI name validation and screenshot.

  • VPF/GPF/SIP/Society/Investment deductions: UW to assess based on 3 months.

  • Rental income lower of 80% of 6 months average or 15% of average 3 months NTH.
  • Half-yearly / annual bonus not allowed. Quarterly incentive allowed if seen for 2 quarters.
